Background information on Prescribe Culture
The foundational social prescribing initiative at the University, Prescribe Culture, came into being in 2019. During COVID-19 lockdowns it converted to online delivery but has returned to in-person programmes delivered at heritage and cultural venues and sites across the city of Edinburgh.
