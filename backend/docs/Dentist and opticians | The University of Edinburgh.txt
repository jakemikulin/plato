Register for eye and dental care while you’re studying. There is a dental and optical medical practice located in the central University campus within Potterrow. 
If you are looking for dental treatment, you will need to first register with a dentist. 
Registering with the NHS is more cost effective than choosing private treatment. 
You can find a local NHS surgery on the NHS inform website.
Find a dentist (NHS inform)
Opticians carry out eye examinations and provide advice on eye health, glasses and contact lenses. Eye tests are free for people in Scotland. 
You can find an optician on the NHS inform website.
Find a local opticians (NHS inform)
