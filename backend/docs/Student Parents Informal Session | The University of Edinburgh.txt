Information on student parents sessions
On the 28th of October 2021 the Students’ Association in collaboration with the Institute for Academic Development (IAD) held an informal online session for student parents. It was called 'Finding a balance: student and parenting responsibilities'. 
Three presenters (including the Student Parents Representative) shared their experiences and tips. The recording of the session can be watched here:   https://media.ed.ac.uk/media/Student+Parents+Session-20211028_100342-Meeting+Recording/1_t7goe6cu  and a slide with useful resources can be found below.
